Complete Slow Roads Guide

Everything you need to know for the perfect driving experience

Basic Controls

Driving Controls

  • WASD Keys: Drive forward, backward, left, right
  • Arrow Keys: Alternative driving controls
  • Shift: Boost/Accelerate
  • Space: Brake
  • B: Handbrake
  • R: Reset to road

Camera Controls

  • Mouse Drag: Look around
  • C: Switch camera view
  • Scroll: Zoom in/out
  • Right Click + Drag: Pan camera

Environment Controls

Weather & Time

  • Q: Previous weather condition
  • E: Next weather condition
  • 1-9: Quick weather selection
  • Mouse Wheel: Change time of day

Vehicle Settings

  • H: Toggle headlights
  • P: Pause game
  • F: Toggle fullscreen
  • Esc: Open settings menu

Optimal Settings Guide

Visual Quality Settings

For Best Performance: Start with Medium quality and adjust based on your device's performance.

  • Low: 30-60 FPS on older devices
  • Medium: 60+ FPS on most modern devices
  • High: 60+ FPS on gaming PCs
  • Ultra: 60+ FPS on high-end gaming rigs

Camera & FOV Settings

Field of View (FOV): Adjust based on your preference and screen size.

  • Low FOV (60-70): Better for speed and precision driving
  • Medium FOV (80-90): Balanced view, good for most players
  • High FOV (100+): Better for scenery appreciation and wide screens

Steering Sensitivity

Keyboard Users: Lower sensitivity (0.3-0.6) for more precise control

Controller Users: Higher sensitivity (0.7-1.0) for responsive steering

Audio Settings Tips

For Relaxation

  • Lower engine volume (20-40%)
  • Higher ambient sounds (60-80%)
  • Enable wind and nature sounds
  • Disable or lower music volume

For Immersion

  • Balanced engine and ambient (50-60%)
  • Enable all sound effects
  • Moderate music volume (30-50%)
  • Use headphones for best experience

Driving Tips for Maximum Relaxation

Perfect Your Driving Style

  • Slow and Steady: Don't rush - enjoy the journey
  • Use Cruise Control: Let the car maintain speed on straight roads
  • Explore Side Roads: Take detours to discover new scenery
  • Change Perspectives: Switch camera angles for different views
  • Weather Matching: Choose weather that matches your mood

Performance Optimization

Browser Settings

  • Close unnecessary tabs
  • Disable heavy browser extensions
  • Use hardware acceleration
  • Clear browser cache regularly

System Settings

  • Close background applications
  • Ensure stable internet connection
  • Update graphics drivers
  • Use a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Game Won't Load

  • Check your internet connection
  • Try refreshing the page
  • Clear browser cache and cookies
  • Disable ad blockers temporarily
  • Try a different browser

Poor Performance

  • Lower visual quality settings
  • Reduce FOV if too wide
  • Close other applications
  • Check if hardware acceleration is enabled
  • Try fullscreen mode
